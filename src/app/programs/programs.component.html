YOUTH PROGRAM

CONTROL-FOCUS-FUN

Our youth program is divided into three sections:

  • Cat Kids (ages 5-9)
  • Junior Panthers (ages 9-12)
  • Panthers (ages 12-15)
    •

The youth program takes students from white belt through junior black belt. In the first two stages of learning, our classes emphasize:

  • strong boudaries against bully behavior
  • respect for classmates
  • training partner safety
  • mental focus
  • sustained attention
  • athletic work-ethic
  • growth mindset

As students becomes more confident in the basic movements and skills of the martial arts, we teach them to verbally, and if necessary, physically stand against bully aggression and use their self-assurance to take on positive leadership roles in the dojo, in school, and in their community.
